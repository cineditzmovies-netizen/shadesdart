About Our Artists

We are a small collective of passionate artists, united by our love for colors, textures, and the emotions that only a painting can convey. Each canvas reflects a personal story, a moment, or a dream — brought to life with dedication and imagination.

Sandinu S

Sandinu’s art celebrates nature’s quiet rhythm. His pieces often capture soft, flowing landscapes with pastel tones and delicate brushstrokes.

Watheesha A

Watheesha draws inspiration from Sri Lanka’s sunsets and oceans, merging realism and abstraction to explore harmony and light.

Kawya S

Kawya is known for bold, expressive colors that depict emotion through movement. Her art reflects both strength and vulnerability.
